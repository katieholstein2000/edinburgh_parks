The Meadows
The Meadows is a park near the University of Edinburgh campus. It is frequented by students all year round and a favorite of those looking to have a picnic in the summer. The Meadows are especially pretty during spring when the cherry blossoms bloom or in autumn when the leaves change color. The Meadows is also located close to the Quartermile area, which offers lovely cafes and shops. The Meadows offers biking and walking lanes as well as benches.
