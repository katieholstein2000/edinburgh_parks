Prince's Street Gardens
Prince's Street is at the center of Edinburgh, offering a wide variety of shops and restaurants. In a valley next to Prince's Street lies a beautiful garden with statues, the Ross Fountain, and benches. The gardens offer great views of Edinburgh Castle and are also next to the Scott Monument and the Scottish National Gallery.
